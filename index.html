Where are they from...

A story into refugees, wars, belligerent countries, torn countries, their neighbouring countries and so on through UNHCR's refugee data finder. Data provide a limited scope, but the true stories are happending in their land every day.

Every year, there are people fleeing from their homes and seeking protection of other countries due to conflicts, wars and persecution.

This map shows how many refugee were admitted by UNHCR refugee program under the organization's mandate. The data covers nearly six decades from 1961, the beginning of Vietnam war to 2019.

The UNHCR is in charge of resettlement with the help of participating countries, such as the US, Canada, Germany, the United Kingdom, Australia and the Nordic countires. Among these countries, the US has been the world's top resettlement country.

While European countries, the US and Canada have traditionally taken on an important role in global refugee resettlement, countries and regions in Asia and Africa have seen a growing number of refugees seeking asylum since 1980s.
